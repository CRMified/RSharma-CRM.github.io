About NGLCC

NGLCC (sample) is the largest certifying body and advocacy organization for LGBT-owned businesses. We partner with governments and corporations to create access, growth, and opportunity for diverse entrepreneurs.

Nonprofit • Supplier Diversity

Programs & Services

Certification

LGBTBE® certification verifies business ownership and helps connect suppliers to corporate sourcing programs.

Advocacy

We influence procurement policies and fight for inclusive economic policy at local and national levels.

Business Growth

Mentorship, capacity building, and corporate matchmaking to scale operations and revenue.
